Privacy Policy

StackStatsGeo · Last updated: July 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the StackStatsGeo app (the "App") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

  • Account information you provide: name, email address, phone number, and optionally your address, city, state, and postal code.
  • Poker session data you enter: sessions, games, venues, buy-ins, results, and related notes and statistics.
  • Location data: with your permission, your device location is used to suggest nearby poker rooms and tag sessions. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.
  • Purchase information: when you subscribe, the App receives a transaction identifier and subscription status from the app store. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain your account and the App's features.
  • To record and display your poker session history and statistics.
  • To process and validate subscriptions and unlock premium access.
  • To suggest nearby poker rooms based on your location (with permission).
  • To communicate with you about your account or support requests.

How Information Is Shared

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with:

  • Apple — to process and verify in-app subscription purchases and renewals on iOS.
  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe on non-iOS platforms) — to process subscription payments.
  • Service providers that host our servers and infrastructure, bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Authorities, where required by law or to protect our legal rights.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and associated data at any time from the App's Account screen ("Delete Account"). Deletion is irreversible.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and hashed passwords. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 17 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact
